INSTITUTE PORTAL

About

An Institute Management Portal that will overlook the activities going inside the institution without manual processing. All information will be updated automatically by using the information stored in the database by providing a GUI interface to the end user.
The main motive behind this is to develop a system which will able to handle overall tasks going inside the institutions without much effort.

Student Management

Maintains student life-cycle for their entire academic curriculum.

Parents Communication

Parents get information regarding current academic progress from the statistical reports of the child.

Staff Management

helpful to keep track of employee status. Activities carried out on regular basis are also maintained.

Result Processing

Result Processing Module integrated with Pre-Examination Processes and Examination Processes.

Hostel Management

Managing hostel related issues and complaints and also to manage student accommodations.

Mass E-mail System

Useful for sending emails in bulk and also single. Students can get alert through the mails.
